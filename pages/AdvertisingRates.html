SoCal Television Production Standards for Submitted Programming / Advertisements:
All programs/advertisements submitted to IETV must meet the following standards: No pornography, No obscene gestures or language use, and No illegal drug use in your advertisements and/or programs will be accepted. The IETV programming staff reserves the right to reject any program if it does not meet our IETV guidelines.  Programs/advertisements must have proper NTSC audio and video levels with adequate lighting.  Each program/advertisement must have 10 seconds of black screen before and 10 seconds of black screen after the program/advertisement, with NO color bars, NO slate, NO countdown.
SoCal Television Production Standards for 10-second freeze-Frame Advertisements:
If you decide to create your own 10-second freeze-frame advertisement remember the following:
  • Freeze-frame must have a one-inch surrounding border.
  • The smallest font to be used is 40, for proper viewing purposes.
  • Use an easy to read, bold font, on a background that will provide a strong contrast to your text, in order to read your message clearly.
